I Need to Write a Crocodile

The Fast-Paced Improv Typing Game

I Need To Write A Crocodile is the quick, exciting, off the cuff creative writing game of HackRice!

Your mission: you have three minutes to write a story. At the beginning of the game, you will receive three prompts: a noun, an action, and a setting. You must write a story including these three words verbatim. In addition, every 10, 15, or 20 seconds, depending on the difficulty level, you will receive another prompt to help get your creative juices flowing. The goal: to write a story using as many of the prompts as you can!

Try this game and then share it with your friends and family to see who is the most creative! :)

Created by Charles Paul, Shelby Bice, Rae Holcomb, Jennifer Hellar, and Madison Lewis for HackRice 2016
